Whereas, farmers from Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh, and Mizoram have asked the minimum number of questions. Month-wise insights
The order of months with the increasing number of query calls in the dataset is noted to be: May, February, November, April, December, January, March, June, October, August, July, and September.
